About Me

I'm a classical art apprentice, currently learning and exploring various traditional techniques. I work under the mentorship of Sadie Valerie. I also attend Aristides Atelier from Juliette Aristides.

My journey as an artist is driven by a deep passion for classical art, and this site is where I share my progress, research, and creations.
